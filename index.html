Frequently Asked Questions

How does the process work to sell my house?

Once you have completed the Get My Offer Form, one of our real estate solutions specialists will contact you shortly (usually within 24 to 48 hours). In some situations, we will need to gather additional information. We will research your property and discuss all the details with you. We may be able to make you an offer for our company to buy your home directly from you right over the phone, or in most cases we will schedule a time with you to view the property and make you an offer.

What type of offer will I receive on my home through the Guarenteed Offer program?

A Guaranteed Offer from Circle Homes is the most competitive all-cash offer in the industry. Circle Homes is dedicated to helping our clients sell fast and reach their real estate goals. A Guaranteed Offer makes selling your home easy.

What sort of houses do you buy?

We buy properties in ay conditio or situation. We want to make the process easy and straightforward so you don't have to go through different showings and appraisals. We want to buy it. We can advise you on the best approach to Get My Offer quickly within your unique situation. We buy houses in any condition, regardless of why they are for sale.

How does the Guaranteed Offer process work?

Once you submit your home for a Guaranteed Offer, our team will verify if your home meets the program parameters. If your home meets the requirements, a Guarenteed Offer will be extended, contingent upon a walkthrough to collect an interior assessment conducted by Circle Homes. Finally, upon accepting the offer, you have the opporunity to close in as little as 21 days.

Are you Realtors?

Circle Homes is a real estate investment and solution company. We are property acquisition specialists that buy houses directly from sellers. We want to buy your home. There is never a charge or a commission when we buy your property! However, if listing your property is the best solution then we can, one of our members hold an active FL real estate license and we can proceed.
